# Speech model notice

The bundled English speech clips were generated locally with
[Kokoro-82M](https://huggingface.co/hexgrad/Kokoro-82M), using its built-in
`af_heart` American female voice.

- Model license: Apache-2.0
- Locale: `en-US`
- Delivery format: mono 24 kHz AAC
- The model itself is not distributed with the app.

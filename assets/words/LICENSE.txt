Word-picture illustrations in this directory are cached from public Iconify collections.
Most are derived from Microsoft Fluent Emoji; exact source collection and icon names
for every asset are recorded in sources.json.

Source: https://github.com/microsoft/fluentui-emoji
Retrieved through the Iconify API: https://api.iconify.design/

Additional collections used for word-family nouns:
- Huge Icons — MIT — https://icon-sets.iconify.design/hugeicons/
- Material Design Icons — Apache-2.0 — https://github.com/Templarian/MaterialDesign
- Röntgen — CC-BY-4.0 — https://github.com/enzet/Roentgen
- Game Icons — CC-BY-3.0 — https://github.com/game-icons/icons
- Lucide Lab — ISC — https://github.com/lucide-icons/lucide-lab
- Material Symbols — Apache-2.0 — https://github.com/google/material-design-icons
